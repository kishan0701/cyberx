Hardware Security Gadgets

Essential physical security tools to protect your hardware, network, and offline data from digital intruders.

Hardware Security Key

Physical keys like YubiKey provide cryptographic two-factor authentication (2FA) that is immune to phishing attacks.

Encrypted USB Drive

USB drives with built-in hardware PIN pads and military-grade AES 256-bit encryption keep your sensitive files private.

Hardware Firewall Router

A dedicated VPN router runs network-wide encryption, protecting all connected IoT smart-home devices at the gateway.

USB Data Blocker

Also known as a "USB Condom", it blocks data pins on USB ports, letting power flow while preventing juice jacking.

Webcam Cover & Mic Blocker

Physical webcam slides and mic blockers physically disable visual and audio spying even if spyware infects your device.

Privacy Screen Filter

Polarized filters for laptops and phones narrow the viewing angle to block shoulder surfing in public places.
